AKAN NAMES

The Akan naming system is a cultture that was developed by Ganaians. Children were given a day name which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. The tradition was shared though-out west Africa and the African Diaspora. Did you know that Ghana was the only country in the African continent that got independence in a peaceful way and was led by Kwame Nkruma,who happens to be Ghana's first president.
